Human Resources Absenteeism

Introduction

The HR department noticed a drop in performance from the team and because absenteeism is quite a problem We would be investigating the causes and determine ways to help those who are missing work.

Problem

We analysed the data, studied it and gathered insights.

Recommendation

Our Suggestion was to get accommodation with close proximity to their office We also adviced them to inspect the office environment to make sure it is conducive for all staff as this can also affect medical health.
