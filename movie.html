Movie Recommender System

Introduction

The world today is technology driven, recommender systems are humanely and financially pivotal in ensuring that people can make smart decisions about the information with which they interact on a regular basis. Particularly in movie recommendations, intelligent algorithms excel in providing movie content recommendations, especially when dealing with the vast array of options available. It allows viewers to discover exceptional titles amidst tens of thousands of choices

Background Context

They were having difficulties in recommending movies to their users. Our task was to develop a recommendation algorithm using either content-based or collaborative filtering techniques. This algorithm is capable of accurately predicting how a user would rate a movie they haven't seen before, based on their past viewing preferences.
