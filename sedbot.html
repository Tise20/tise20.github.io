Student Engagement Discord Bot

Introduction

Limited data is currently captured about students. This makes it hard to tailor marketing content to their unique needs. The more we know about student's lived experiences, the better we can tailor our marketing and learning experiences.

Problem

The project aims to create an interactive Discord bot for students, engaging them with themed weekly questions. Valuable student data gathered will inform tailored marketing content and learning experiences.
